Harry Cookie Website
A modern, responsive bakery website for Harry Cookie - featuring our delicious Classico Collection of freshly baked cookies.
About
Harry Cookie is a bakery specializing in premium cookies, offering a delightful selection of flavors that bring happiness in every nibble. Our website showcases our product line and provides customers with an easy way to explore our offerings.
Features

Responsive Design - Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
Product Showcase - Display of our Classico Collection with pricing
About Page - Information about our bakery and what makes us special
Services Page - Details about our offerings and delivery options
Animated Elements - Smooth animations using WOW.js and Animate.css
Modern UI - Clean, professional design with Bootstrap 5
